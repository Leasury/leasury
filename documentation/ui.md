## Barvy

**Primary Backgrounds (The "Off-Whites")**

- **#FAF9F5** (Light / Cream Background) – This is the warm, paper-like off-white often associated with their "Digital Zen" aesthetic.
- **#F0EFEA** (Cararra) – A slightly cooler/greyer off-white variant sometimes used.
- **#E8E6DC** (Light Gray) – Used for subtle backgrounds and UI elements.

**Primary Text & Dark Elements**

- **#141413** (Dark / Cod Gray) – Used for primary text and dark backgrounds; it is a very dark charcoal rather than pure black.
- **#B0AEA5** (Mid Gray) – Used for secondary elements and borders.

**Accent Colors (Muted/Pastel Tones)**

- **#D97757** (Muted Orange / Terracotta)
- **#6A9BCC** (Muted Blue)
- **#788C5D** (Sage Green)
- **#CC785C** (Antique Brass) – A variation of the terracotta accent.
